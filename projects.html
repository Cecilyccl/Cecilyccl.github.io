International Arbitration

Commercial Arbitration Advocacy Training

Through the CIETAC Cup, the Willem C. Vis Moot team, and the Vis APAC competition, Xueting has developed advanced skills in memorandum writing, issue analysis, procedural argument, and oral advocacy within international commercial arbitration.

Highlights: National Second Prize at the CIETAC Cup; Global Top 16 at Vis APAC.

Legal Translation

High-Level Translation and Legal Language Practice

A sustained line of competition work in legal translation and legal English has sharpened her ability to move accurately between legal systems, rhetorical traditions, and linguistic registers.

Highlights: National Champion in creative translation; National Runner-Up in legal translation; major awards in legal English and business debate.

Research

Comparative Study on the Governance of Facial Recognition

As part of a national research project, Xueting examined U.S. legal materials relevant to facial recognition governance, organized comparative findings, and produced independent research reports to support broader doctrinal analysis.

Focus: comparative legal governance, technology regulation, and transnational legal research methods.
